ION Phantom
Ion is the most futuristic skin in valorant, it uses a futuristic palette of white and blue, with a core at its center fueling the charge. Every time you reload the weapon, you have a reload animation that replaces the core with a new one. It sounds great and pulses geometric shapes when you shoot or equip it. And its finisher explodes the enemy in a spherical electro-pulse.
